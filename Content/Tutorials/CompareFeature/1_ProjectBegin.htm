Beginning a Project

There is no formal definition of 'project' for IFTDSS, rather, the term refers to all the landscape files, model runs, and associated data for your specific area or project. Before beginning, create a folder for your project in My Workspace, to help keep track of your files as they are generated. To create a folder in My Workspace:

Follow the steps below and name your folder: 'Haypress Creek Project'

You may create a folder by selecting the Create Folder option at the top of the My Workspace page.

Give your folder a name and select Submit.

Your new folder will now be visible in the Folders list.

The folder will now be available anytime IFTDSS prompts you to select a folder to store your files.
