Why use IFTDSS for fuels planning?

The IFTDSS online application is designed to make the fuels planning and management process easier by:

  • Providing place to store and edit your landscape and run models online, from any computer, anywhere
  • Integrating many common fire behavior and effects models in a single user interface
  • Linking to common reporting databases to more easily access and submit planning information
  • Exporting results, reports, tables, and files for use in the fuels planning documentation and further analysis

Over the span of 2017, 2018, and beyond, additional capabilities will be added to IFTDSS with the goal of eventually offering all the tools needed by the fire management community to conduct fire and fuels planning
