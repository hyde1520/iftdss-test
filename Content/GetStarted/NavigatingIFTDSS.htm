IFTDSS Overview

There are four main components of the IFTDSS application. These can be accessed anytime, depending upon what you are looking to do, and all of them are available when you first log in, they include:

  • My Workspace: This is a list of all your files used in IFTDSS. These can be organized into folders, and clicking on the name of any file will bring up options such as edit, delete, and view on map. When you first log in, My Workspace is available from the 'Continue Working' button. The files available in My Workspace include:
    • Landscape files (.lcp files) which is the basic file used throughout IFTDSSIFTDSS to represent your landscape.
    • Shapefiles that you upload and those that you create within IFTDSS
    • Model Inputs
    • Model Outputs
  • My Modeling Playground: This is where you select which models you'd like to run, and then begin modeling. All model outputs are also listed in My Modeling Playground. Clicking on a model output name will bring up options such as edit, delete, view, Request a report (see results as tables and charts), or map (see results displayed on a map). You can get to this part of IFTDSS by clicking the Model Fire Behavior/Effects button from the log in screen.
  • Map Studio: This is the mapping interface of IFTDSS, where you can create and edit landscape and shape files, view modeling outputs spatially, and compare various landscape features and modeling outputs across your landscape. This is available on navigation bar at the top of IFTDSS.
  • The Fuels Planning Cycle: The planning cycle walks you through the steps of landscape planning, such as landscape evaluation, strategic planning, implementation, and reporting. Each stage of the planning cycle (2 of 5 are currently operational in this version of IFTDSS) walks you through the steps necessary to complete it. Each step will take you to the part of the IFTDSS application needed to complete it. The Planning Cycle is available from the 'Start Here' button when you first log into IFTDSS.

Getting help: short help tips are available throughout most of IFTDSS, displayed on the right-hand side of the page. Full length help, videos, and walkthroughs like this one, are linked to each part of IFTDSS and available from the top navigation by selecting Help>About This Page. The Help>HelpCenter option will bring you to search-bar to search help contents.

Log Off of IFTDSS: You may log off of your IFTDSS session at any time by selecting your account name in the top right of the top menu bar, and selecting the Logout option at the bottom of the dropdown menu.
